## What are morning pages?

I sit down in my private place with a cup of dark coffee. A pile of blan A4s in front of me and my fav fountain pen in my hand. I start a timer for 22 minutes. Now I write down EVERYTHING that pops into my head.

## There's no wrong way to do morning pages

There's no wrong way for me to do my morning pages. And doing morning pages is not writing. I'm just moving my pen across the page. Writing whatever. As fast as I can. Nothing is too weird, angry, evil, stupid or politically incorrect for my morning pages. Because nobody is EVER reading my morning pages. I didn't even read them myself the first handful of weeks.
